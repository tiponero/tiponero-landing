The Problem

Accepting Monero is harder than it should be.

Most setups paste a static address and call it done. That means every payer sends to the same address, you have no way to attribute payments, and you are not using subaddresses the way Monero was designed to work.

You could build the plumbing yourself. Or you could use Tiponero.

How It Works

Make it simple for your payers.

01

Deploy

Deploy on your own server. Point it at your monero-wallet-rpc instance running in view-only mode. Your spending keys never touch the server.

02

Embed

Embed the widget on your site. A single iframe drop-in or SVG badge. Works on any website or markdown document.

03

Use Subaddress

Each payer gets a unique subaddress. Displayed as a QR code, monitored in real time. Address is never reused.

04

Track Payments

Payments appear in your admin panel. Track transactions, manage widgets. Your data stays on your machine.

Built for people who take privacy seriously.

01
Open source developers
Accept donations for your project without routing payments through a third-party custodian. Keep your donors and your amounts to yourself.
02
Journalists and activists
Financial surveillance is real. Monero solves it at the protocol level. Tiponero makes sure your payment setup doesn't introduce it back at the application layer.
03
Privacy-conscious creators
Writers, podcasters, independent researchers. If you want to accept support from your audience without handing your financial data to a platform, this is for you.
04
Vendors and merchants
Sell digital goods, services, or access without a payment processor that knows your customers. Fixed-amount requests, automatic redirect on confirmation.

Unique subaddress per transaction

The Monero protocol was built around subaddresses. Tiponero uses them correctly: one per payment session, never reused.

visibility_off

View-key only

Your monero-wallet-rpc runs in view-only mode. The server can monitor incoming funds and cannot spend them. Ever.

tune

Two modes, one widget

Donation mode accepts arbitrary amounts. Vendor mode requires a fixed amount before confirming.

open_in_new

Redirect on completion

Define a redirect URL per widget. When a payment is detected, the user is redirected automatically. Confirmation pages, order receipts, thank-you flows. Yours to control.

widgets

Embeddable widget

Drop an iframe into any page. A unique QR code is generated per payment, with real-time payment detection. Works on any stack.

dashboard

Admin panel

Track transactions, manage multiple widgets, and monitor your setup from a self-hosted private dashboard.
